YouthMappers Bangladesh Summit 2025

YouthMappers Bangladesh Summit is the annual event for all mappers and OpenStreetMap users. In 2025 the YouthMappers Bangladesh Summit will be a two day on-site summit taking place in Patuakhali Science and Technology University, Patuakhali.

The local team is committed to providing participants with a safe conference environment in accordance with the latest safety and health guidelines set by authorities.
